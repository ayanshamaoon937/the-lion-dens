Biography

Charlie Parsons is an online broadcasting personality and content creator, most notably in his role as the creative driving force behind sports media platform The Stomping Ground.

Through collaboration with the biggest names across the sporting world, Charlie crafts compelling live event coverage, commentary, exclusive interviews, and behind-the-scenes insight into the world of sport for his viewers across multiple platforms.

Charlie has worked with a number of notable brand partners in the past across his various platforms and channels, through which he is routinely seeing 1,000,000+ monthly views.
